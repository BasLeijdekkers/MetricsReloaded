Calculates the level order of an package. The level order is defined as 0 for packages which are dependent on no other project packages. Packages with dependencies have level order equal to one more then the greatest dependency class, ignoring packages on which they have mutual or cyclic dependencies. Conceptually, level order measure how many "layers" of software a package depends on. Packages with large level orders may be difficult to test and maintain. In designs with large cyclic dependencies, level order may give an unreasonably rosy picture of how many layers of software depends on.
Abbreviation: Level
