Calculates on the degree of cohesiveness of a class. This is on of the variants of Lack of cohesion in methods metric. The metric is the mean number of methods accessing each field minus the number of methods over 1 minus the number of methods. For more information see Gabriela Czibula, Zsuzsanna Marian, Istvan Gergely Czibula (2015) Detecting software design defects using relational association rule mining
