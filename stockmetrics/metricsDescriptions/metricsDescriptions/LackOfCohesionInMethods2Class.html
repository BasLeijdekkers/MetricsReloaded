Calculates the degree of cohesiveness of a class. This is one of the variants of Lack of cohesion in methods metric. The metric is the mean number of methods accessing each field minus the number of methods divided by 1 minus the number of methods. For more information see Henderson-Sellers, B., Object-oriented metrics: measures of complexity, Prentice-Hall, pp.142-147, 1996.
Abbreviation: LCOM2
