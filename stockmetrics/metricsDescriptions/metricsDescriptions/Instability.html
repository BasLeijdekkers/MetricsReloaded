Calculates the Instability of a package. The Instability of a package is defined as the package's Efferent Couplings divided by the sum of the package's Afferent and Efferent Couplings (Ce√∑(Ca+Ce)). This metric has a range of [0,1]. I=0 indicates a maximally stable package, I=1 indicates a maximally unstable package.

For more information on the Instability metric read OO Design Quality Metrics by Robert C. Martin.
