Calculates the instability of a package. The instability of a package is defined as the package's efferent couplings divided by the sum of the package's afferent and efferent couplings (Ce√∑(Ca+Ce)). This metric has a range of [0,1], 0 indicates a maximally stable category, 1 indicates a maximally unstable category. For more information on the instability metric and how it is used, see http://www.objectmentor.com/resources/articles/oodmetrc.pdf.
